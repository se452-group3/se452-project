Who Are We?

We are a small start up company in the ChicagoLand area and we are just a group of everyday ordinary people who love dogs and want to connect with more people just like us. So, we decided to make a company centered around dogs. To start that process, we started coding this web site. From there it went from something that was originally a passion project to something that we can monetize. Come and join us and other dog lovers in an interesting endeavor that allows for you to have someone care for your little buddy while you are away in a different location. This is truly the start of something special! Don't be the one left out of all the fun!

Our Mission

Our mission is to make it easier for you to travel knowing that your dog is in good hands. While you are away for work or play, a fellow dog lover is there to help take care of your dog. The ability to connect people in their same area is something that is special for all dog lovers.
